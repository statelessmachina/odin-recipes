Chicken soup

Description:

This delicious soup will make your mouth water and your heart yearn for home. The soup has 3 main ingredients and is meant to be simple.

Ingredients

There are three basic ingredients.

  • Chicken: 3 lbs. whole chicken.
  • Vegetables: Carrots, celery, and onion. If you prefer a heartier soup, may add some root vegetables, such as potato, squash, or beets.
  • Seasonings:Simple salt and pepper mix to taste.

Steps

  1. Chop the vegetables into bite-sized pieces.
  2. Mix the chopped vegetables and the chicken in a pot and fill with water about 3/4 full.
  3. Cover the pot and boil until the meat is falling off the bone.
  4. Remove the chicken from the pot and separate the meat from the bones.
  5. Shred the meat and discard the skin and bones.
  6. Place the shredded chicken back into the pot and simmer the soup for approximately 1 more hour.
  7. Season the soup to taste.
